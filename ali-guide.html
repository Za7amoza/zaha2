Interview Questions — Ali's Exact Angles

"Walk me through a bank reconciliation."
Describe your actual monthly process from the 6-month role — the system used, how you identified and resolved discrepancies.
"How do you handle an invoice discrepancy?"
3-way matching process from your AP work — purchase order, delivery note, invoice verification.
"What's the difference between IFRS and GAAP?"
Use your ACCA IFRS cert knowledge directly — IAS 16 (PPE), IFRS 15 (Revenue), IFRS 16 (Leases), conceptual framework. This is where the cert pays off.
"How does Python help in accounting?"
"I automate recurring tasks — reconciliation scripts, aging reports — so I spend time on analysis, not data entry."
"Why do you want to work in the Gulf?" Gulf only
"IFRS is the standard there. I want to work in an environment where my certification is baseline, not a niche skill — and where I can grow with professionals who take international standards seriously."
